Privacy

This guide is a personal, non-commercial field guide. Accounts exist for one reason: so you can save favourite plants and have them follow you between your phone, tablet, and computer. Here’s exactly what that involves.

What we collect

  • Your email address — used only to sign you in (we email you a one-time link) and to attach your saved plants to you. There are no passwords.
  • Your favourites — the list of plants you’ve tapped the ♥ on.

That’s the whole list. No name, no location, no payment details, no browsing history, no ads, no third-party trackers, no analytics profiles.

Where it’s stored

Sign-in and the favourites list are handled by Supabase, a hosting service acting as our data processor. Your favourites are protected by per-user security rules so that only you (when signed in) can read or change your own list. The plant pages, photos, and care notes themselves are public and need no account.

What we don’t do

We don’t sell, rent, or share your information. We don’t use it for advertising or send marketing email. The only email you’ll ever get is the sign-in link you asked for.

Your choices

You can sign out at any time from the link in the top corner. You can delete your saved data below — that removes every favourite tied to your account and signs you out. To have the account itself (your email record) erased entirely, email the address below and we’ll remove it.

You’re signed out — there’s no saved data on this device.

Cookies & local storage

When you sign in, your browser keeps a sign-in token in its local storage so you stay signed in between visits. That’s the only thing stored on your device, and signing out clears it. We set no advertising or tracking cookies.

Children

This guide isn’t directed at children under 13, and accounts aren’t intended for them.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will appear on this page.
